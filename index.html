Belief system

No bullshit. I say what I mean and I mean what I say, I expect the same from others.

Default to open. I worked hard on myself to develop an open/growth mindset, I lead with questions and curiosity, and will assume positive intent.

Outcome over output. I don't care how many lines of code you write, how many meetings you attend, how many tickets you close, I care about your impact on the team and product. See Martin Fowler's article.

Productivity isn't everything. I care about sustainable productivity, not burnout. I believe in working smart, taking breaks, and maintaining a healthy work-life balance. Getting lost isn't productive but often leads to learning new things, getting different perspectives. We're on this earth for a limited time and I believe we should be here to enjoy it, not maximize shareholder value at all cost (no 996 for me).

Keep It Simple, Stupid. Intrinsic complexity is unavoidable, accidental complexity is not. I strive to build simple systems based on domain understanding, with strong observability and the least surprises. I'm keen on a very lightweight domain driven approach to ensure all stakeholders share the same ubiquitous language.
